Who We Are


"We started Ki Sushi & Sake Bar was in 2006. To us, 'Ki' means giving, and we were inspired by the book, 'The Giving Tree' . 'The Giving Tree' is a magical story of a loving tree who always provides a boy what he needs. We named our restaurant 'Ki' , and we wanted to provide our customers good food that feels homemade. Our goal is to keep improving the quality of the food until every guest can be satisfied."

- Joanne and David, Owners
